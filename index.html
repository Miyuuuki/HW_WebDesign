ELECTIONS 2022

About Elections 2022

On May 9, 2022, registered voters will cast their ballots to elect officials who will fill executive and legislative posts at the national, provincial and local levels. In total, the Filipino people shall elect into office:

  • President
  • Vice president
  • Senators
  • Members of the House of Representatives from district and party-list groups
  • Members of provincial boards
  • Provincial governors and vice-governors
  • Mayors and vice-mayors of cities and municipalities
  • Councilors

Elections for the positions of barangay captain, kagawad, and the sangguniang kabataan will be held on December 5, 2022 per R.A. 11462.
